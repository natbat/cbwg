California Bat Working Group Email Mailing List (listserv) Information

To join the CBWG listserv so that you can send and receive emails within the state working group, you may contact Joe Szewczak (joe@humboldt.edu) or follow the instructions below to sign up directly. (Also the adorable Hoary bat photo above was taken by Joe.)

Listserv emails have included announcements on training workshops, conferences, and symposiums, permitting changes and requirements, meetings, and equipment for sale; discussions have included pros and cons about equipment, bat and researcher health news, news articles, and media links; research news; and submission requests for California news to add to the Western Bat Working Group newsletter.

The California Bat Working Group Listserv is open to everyone that would like to send and receive information and news regarding bats, and for disseminating news and events for the CBWG.

Subscribe to the CBWG mailing list

To receive list mail at the address from which you're sending your mail, simply send a message to: joe@humboldt.edu and write in the subject of the message: subscribe CBWGList

If for some reason you wish to have the mailings go to a different address then tell Joe that address in the body of the message.

Once subscribed, you should next get a welcome message, containing list policies and features.

Unsubscribe from the mailing list

To unsubscribe from the mailing list, simply send a message to: joe (at the domain) humboldt.edu and write in the subject of the message write: unsubscribe CBWGList.

Use the mailing list

To send a message to EVERYONE on the list: CBWGlist (at the domain) humboldt.edu

Please honor the usual etiquette for listservs:

  • Do not post a message unless you think it may be of interest to the entire group.
  • Use care in replying to a message, e.g., if someone posts a message and you want to reply to that individual only, check that the address on your message is to that individual and not the mailing list address.
